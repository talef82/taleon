HIGHLIGHTS

I'm Jon Flores, the mind and soul behind the Taleon project. Based in Spain, I've got serious skin in the game: I've been behind the decks as a DJ since 1996 and crafting tracks in the studio since 2002. My journey in the industry also includes founding my own label, Undercool Productions, back in 2009.

After years of musical evolution, I launched my first single under the Taleon alias in 2022, focusing my sound entirely on Organic, Progressive, and Melodic House.

My ultimate goal is simple: to share music that is undeniably danceable, yet deeply emotional. I want to transmit good vibes through melodies that make your mind travel, provoke thought, or simply become the perfect soundtrack accompanying you through life.
